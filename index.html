About Us

Its my pleasure to inform you that my firm is a bona-fide firm & enlisted with the office of Bangladesh Air Force , Army & Navy " since 25 years as an Indenter , Importer for supply of Varieties of Defence stores e,g Arms and Ammunition, F- 7, PT-6, K-8, Yak-38, AN-32, C-130 Aircraft and MI-17/171 & Bell-212,206,205 Helicopter Engines and Avionics spares including overhauling , Military Ground communication eqpt like HF,VHF&UHF Equipment including spares ,Radar including spares , Microwave Communication Equipment, Tele Communication Eqpt, Trg Aid Equipment , Precision measuring equipment /Test Equipment, Military Vehicles , Ambulance to Bangladesh Army , Air Force & Navy Police, Bangladesh Border Guard, Bangladesh Police and Rapid Action Battalion (RAB), Bangladesh Ordinance Factory (BOF). Besides this the company also supplies to different government and semi government organizations such as City Corporations, Roads and Highways, Bangladesh Petroleum Corporation, Bangladesh Inland Water Transport Authority and Bangladesh Railway etc. It has established itself as a reputed and dependable firm over the years. It was established in the year 1995 and a host of civil and military retired Officers intimately and directly work to develop the business.
